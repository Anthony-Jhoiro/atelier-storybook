A card has an image, a title and a text. Its role is to represent a preview of something and maybe suggest an action

### Action

By default, a Card does not have an action button :

## Orientation
You can also specify the card orientation using the `orientation` props.
Yo can give the value `"vertical"` or `"horizontal"`.
It works fine with the action props
